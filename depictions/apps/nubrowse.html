nuBrowse 0.1

a new browser for iOS 4

This is the first iPhone app i've ever made, it's an UIWebView-based browser that can use duckduckgo as it's default browser.

WARNING

You first NEED to go to your WI-FI settings and change your proxy to manual and set this:

Domain: muellers-software.org

Port: 3080

Credentials: None (until it asks you to, once it does you set USER: guest PASS: noindex, after that you go back to your proxy settings and turn credentials on, and then you put the previously mentioned credentials.)
