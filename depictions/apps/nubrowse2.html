nuBrowse2 1.0

A tabbed browser for iOS 4

nuBrowse2 is a tabbed web browser for jailbroken iOS 4.2+. Features tabbed browsing with up to 8 tabs, DuckDuckGo or Wiby search, 7 user-agent presets, bookmarks, history, and desktop mode toggle. All toolbar icons are drawn with Core Graphics.
